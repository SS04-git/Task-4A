PRIVACY POLICY

ILLICIT CONTENT USAGE

Victims often find it challenging to seek legal recourse due to the lack of clear laws addressing the misuse of their likenesses in digital media. The emotional toll on individuals targeted by deepfakes can be severe. Victims report feelings of anxiety, helplessness, and violation of privacy, which can lead to long-term psychological effects. Furthermore, the spread of misinformation through deepfakes can manipulate public perception and erode trust in credible information sources, leading to societal polarization.

MISUSE AND EXPLOITATION

Deepfake technology enables the creation of highly realistic but falsified content, which can be exploited in ways that harm individuals’ personal and professional lives. People face risks to their privacy, reputation, and emotional well-being, as well as challenges in maintaining control over their own digital presence. The technology’s misuse often leads to trust issues, affecting both individuals and society at large.

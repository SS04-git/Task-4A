PRACTICES FOR PROTECTION

PUBLIC AWARENESS CAMPAIGN

Public awareness campaigns aimed at educating users about the risks associated with deepfakes are essential. Users should be trained on how to identify manipulated media and understand the potential consequences of sharing unverified content.

POLICY IMPLEMENTATION

Implementing policies that require explicit consent before using someone’s likeness in a deepfake is essential for safeguarding individual rights and minimizing misuse. Such policies could mandate that creators disclose the use of synthetic media and obtain permission, especially for content that could impact a person’s reputation or privacy. Additionally, organizations should prioritize investing in cybersecurity measures to prevent the unauthorized creation or dissemination of deepfake content. By establishing clear standards and protective measures, these policies would not only protect individuals but also encourage responsible use of deepfake technology, helping to mitigate potential exploitation risks in an increasingly digital world.
